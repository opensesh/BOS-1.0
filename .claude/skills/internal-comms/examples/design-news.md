# Design News Format

## Instructions

You are being asked to create a design news update for Karim and Morgan. These updates help you stay aware of what's happening in design, AI, and the tools you use. They should be inspiring, informative, and quick to read (2 pages max).

The goal is to keep a pulse on:
- **Design + AI industry** (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, Nvidia)
- **Website builders** (Figma, Framer, Webflow, Shopify)
- **Design inspiration** (Awwwards, showcase sites)

## What to Include

### 1. **AI & Tech Updates**
- Major announcements from AI companies
- New tools or features that impact designers
- Industry trends worth knowing

### 2. **Design Tool Updates**
- New features in Figma, Framer, Webflow, Shopify
- Tool releases or updates
- Plugin or integration launches

### 3. **Design Inspiration**
- Cool websites from Awwwards or other showcases
- Design trends you're seeing
- Techniques or styles worth exploring

## Data Sources

Use these to find content:
- **Web Search**: Find latest news, tool updates, announcements
- **Claude Knowledge**: Reference your saved news sources
- **Awwwards**: Search for recent winning sites
- **Company blogs**: Anthropic, Figma, Framer, Webflow blogs
- **Design News**: Designer News, Smashing Magazine, CSS Tricks

## Workflow

1. **Search for AI news**: Check what's new from Anthropic, OpenAI, Google
2. **Check design tools**: Look for Figma, Framer, Webflow updates
3. **Find inspiration**: Browse Awwwards or design showcases
4. **Curate the best**: Pick 5-7 items that are most relevant or interesting
5. **Write it up**: Follow the format below with brief takes

## Style Guidelines

- **Tone**: Casual and enthusiastic. Share what excites you.
- **Length**: 2 pages max. 5-7 items is plenty.
- **Curation**: Quality over quantity - only share what's actually interesting
- **Brevity**: Keep each item to 2-3 sentences
- **Links**: Always include links so you can explore later
- **Relevance**: Focus on things that impact your work or inspire you
- **Variety**: Mix AI news, tool updates, and inspiration

## Tips

- **Check frequency**: Weekly or ad-hoc when there's interesting news
- **Be selective**: You don't need to cover everything - just the highlights
- **Add your take**: Don't just summarize - say why it matters to you
- **Save time**: Use web search and Claude knowledge to find sources quickly
- **Keep a list**: When you see cool sites or news during the week, note them
- **Focus on action**: What can you try? What should you test?
- **Mix categories**: Don't just do AI or just tools - variety keeps it interesting
- **Include visuals**: Link to sites so you can see the work, not just read about it
